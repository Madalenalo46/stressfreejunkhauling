Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Standard rental is 7 days, but we offer flexible terms based on your project needs.

Do I need a permit to place a dumpster on the street?

If you’re placing the dumpster on public property, a permit may be required. We’ll guide you through the process based on local regulations.

What happens if I need another bin?

Call us and we’ll schedule a pickup and replacement right away often within 24 hours.

Where can you place the dumpster on my property?

The ideal location is a flat, solid surface like a driveway. Our expert drivers can place the roll off dumpster precisely to maximize your convenience and protect your property. We need a clear area with no overhead obstructions.

Do I need to be there when you drop it off or pick it up?

No. As long as we have clear instructions on where to place the container and the area is clear of obstructions (like cars), you don’t need to be home. The same goes for pickup.

Can I fill the dumpster to the top?

Not quite. For safety during transport, dumpsters should not be filled above the top edge. We’ll walk you through proper loading practices.
